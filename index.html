    Rubric

    Fixed 0-9 Scoring Guide

    9Extremely Relevant

    Core product match, exact name, primary use, essential keyword.

    8Highly Relevant

    Strong feature, major benefit, or essential attribute.

    7Very Relevant

    Strong secondary match with clear value to the product.

    6Moderately Relevant

    Related and useful, but not core to the product.

    5Partially Relevant

    Mild relation or broader keyword with some connection.

    4Weakly Relevant

    Loose or indirect product connection.

    3Minimally Relevant

    Very weak relation and low conversion value.

    2Marginally Relevant

    Barely related and usually avoidable.

    1Almost Irrelevant

    Very poor match, not enough to describe the product.

    0Completely Irrelevant

    No connection to the product at all.
